The page had no finding — now it leads with one

The maturity ladder was the third chart in section 03 and nothing ever read it out. Computed, it says the thing that matters most about Turkish deposits: 91.3% of the book reprices within three months (demand 37.6% + ≤1m 17.7% + 1–3m 36.0%). That is now a transmission line, a flag with its rule printed, and the reason the TCMB date is on the schedule. Was: a stacked area with no reading.

Movers, Flags, Standings, Ahead — the skeleton the page skipped

Deposits jumped from the vitals straight to "In depth", so a weekly page never told you what moved this week. Movers prints the six vitals week-on-week (the 4-week pace fell 28.5pp in one print — and says why: it is annualized from four weekly numbers). Standings ranks the groups; five rules run, three fire, two print as clear.

The numbered sections go

“01 / 02 / 03 / 04” implied a sequence that isn't one — these are four subjects, not four steps. They are now `SecHead`s with a mono meta line saying the basis (weekly vs monthly), which is the thing a reader actually needs when a page mixes two cadences. Was: Section index="01"… in a boxed Card.

Charts on the sheet, each with its read

Eight charts lose their card chrome and gain finding-titles plus a computed foot line (latest, Δ 52w, leading and trailing group) — so a screenshot still carries the finding. The two lone charts (FX share, LDR) keep the 8/4 register: mark left, read right. Was: ChartCards in a 2- and 3-up grid.

The by-group bar became the standings

`BarByBank` showed one number per group for the latest week — exactly what a standings table says, in less ink and above the fold. No number was dropped; it moved into the brief where it earns its place.
